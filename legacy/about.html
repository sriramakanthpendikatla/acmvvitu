About ACM VVITU

The student chapter of the Association for Computing Machinery at Vasireddy Venkatadri Institute of Technology — building India's next generation of technologists since 2017.

🎯

Our Mission

To advance computing as a science and profession by fostering a community of learners, innovators, and leaders who collaborate on real-world technical challenges.

🔭

Our Vision

To be the most active and impactful student tech chapter in Andhra Pradesh — recognized nationally for hackathons, workshops, and alumni success.

🤝

Our Values

Inclusivity, hands-on learning, peer mentorship, and giving back to the campus community through Hour of Code and outreach programs.
